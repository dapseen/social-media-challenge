How it works

This app is set to reward Social media Ambassadors that are actively sharing the youtube link

  1. Login with facebook to have access to your unique link
  2. Copy your unique link on your My ranking page and send it to people.
  3. Your unique link has all the channels embedded and its very easy for people to click and subscribe
  4. Your number on My ranking page represent people that have successfully subscribed to the channel

This is the first version of Social MEdia challange, and there are loads of features we planning to implement, so bare with us, if you cant find it very easy to use
